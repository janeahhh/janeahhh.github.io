6th grade

In 6th grade I basically had all my friends from 5th grade. I started off as a great year, which was what I expected. In the first semester I had straight A's. Around December, I made 2 absolutely great friends! Around the same time, I lost 2 friends. It was really sad because they were really good friends. I tried to not let it get in the way of grades and it didn't. As the end of the year approached, the friends I lost were coming back and so was I. It was friendships that couldn't stay on bad terms. I made a bunch of friends from different classes of course. These were only major events that happened in the year.

7th grade

In 7th grade I had the same friends and made new ones too. My grades were great as well. I had all A's in the beginning but 2 of my A's turned into B's. I really didn't think much of it but I tried to get them up again. Later in the year, I lost 1 of my new friends and 2 of my older friends. It was rough but I still had more of my really close and awesome friends with me. Even later in the year I maintained a great GPA and attendence. I contributed to projects and completed my work. I always tried my best in classes. The year was coming to an end and was still on top of my academics.

8th grade

So far in 8th grade, my grades have been great. Algebra is challenging. It can be very simple one week but be so confusing the next. I am spending time on my work in algebra so I could understand it. I'm also in the FLA advisory ( Female Leadership Academy ). We are invloved in many school events. For example, for the Veteran's day assembly we made Christmas ornaments ( USA themed ) to hand out to the veteran's who come. I have gained friends and gotten closer with people due to this advisory and also from other classes. It is now quarter 2 and my grades are stable. I am trying my best to keep my grades the best they can be.
